Service

Venue Security Risk Assessments

Before software comes implementation.

SAI also offers venue security risk assessments for hospitality operators seeking to improve safety, compliance, and operational readiness.

Assessments may review:

  • Entry control procedures
  • Barring record processes
  • Security positioning
  • Incident response capability
  • CCTV and evidence workflows
  • Radio and communication systems
  • Staff handover procedures
  • Multi-venue risk exposure

Each assessment is designed to identify operational gaps and provide practical recommendations for venue managers.

This service supports SAI’s wider mission: helping venues turn security problems into structured operational intelligence.
